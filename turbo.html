Experiment No.01

Impact of Jet

Aim :

To verify the momentum equation experimentally through impact of jet experiment.

Description :

The momentum equation based on Newton's 2nd law of motion states that the algebraic sum of external forces applied to control volume of fluid in any direction equal to the rate of change of momentum in that direction. The external forces include the component of the weight of the fluid and of the forces exerted externally upon the boundary surface of control volume.

Experiment No.02

Pelton Wheel Turbine

Aim :

Performance characteristics (output and efficiency variation with speed) for different openings of the nozzle at a constant input head.

Description :

Pelton turbine is a high head impulse turbine. It is used for high head and low flow rate applications. The input pressure head is converted into high velocity jet by means of a nozzle. The jet impinges on the double cupped buckets mounted around the periphery of the runner disc, making the runner to rotate.

Experiment No.03

Francis Turbine

Aim :

To conduct performance test on francis turbine under various heads, speeds and loads and to plot the operating characteristics.

Description :

The Francis turbine is a reaction turbine. The turbine is located between the high pressure water source and the low pressure water exit. The inlet of turbine is in spiral shape. The guide vanes direct the water tangentially to the turbine wheel which is known as a runner. This radial flow of water acts on the runner's vanes, causing the runner to spin.

Experiment No.04

Centrifugal Pump

Aim :

To conduct the performance test on centrifugal pump and to plot the operating characteristics.

Description :

Centrifugal pump is a roto-dynamic machine that imparts energy to fluid by rotating impeller to increase the pressure of a fluid. When the fluid enters the pump impeller along or near to the rotating axis it is accelerated by the impeller, flowing radially outward into a diffuser or volute chamber (casing), from where it exits into the downstream piping system. Centrifugal pumps are used for large discharge through smaller heads.
