Finite Element Analysis

Practical No.01

Analysis of rectangular plate with circular hole

Aim :

To analyze the stress distribution of rectangular plate having a circular hole

Description :

In this analysis, a rectangular geometry with circular hole has been made. A static structural analysis is performed in Ansys. The process involves material selection, meshing, boundary conditions and post processing results.


Practical No.02

Analysis of beam with different cross section and loads

Aim :

To analyze the stress and deflection of beam with different cross section and loads.

Description :

In this analysis, beam with different cross section has been made. Also different loading conditions are applied. A static structural analysis is performed in Ansys. The process involves material selection, meshing, boundary conditions and post processing results.

Practical No.04

Analysis of 3D machine component

Aim :

To analyze a 3D machine component (Knuckle Joint)

Description :

In this analysis, equivalent stress and deflection is calculated. A geometry of Knuckle joint is imported. Knuckle joint is an assembly of single fork, double fork, knuckle pin, collar and tapper pin. A static structural analysis is performed in Ansys. The process involves material selection, meshing, boundary conditions and post processing results.


Practical No.05

Analysis of Truss

Aim :

To analyze the Truss using method of joint.

Description :

The main purpose of a structural analysis on a truss is to determine the internal forces of the members. The member forces are needed for designing the members and joints. A static structural analysis is performed in Ansys. The process involves material selection, meshing, boundary conditions and post processing results.


Practical No.06

Analysis of Spur gear

Aim :

To analyze the spur gear.

Description :

The static analysis of spur gear tooth helps to determine maximum displacement, maximum induced stress and effect of stress variation. A static structural analysis is performed in Ansys. The process involves material selection, meshing, boundary conditions and post processing results.
