CATIA

Description :

CATIA stands for Computer Aided Three-Dimensional Interactive Application. The first release of CATIA was back in 1977 by Dassault Systems, who still maintain and develop the software. It is mostly used for its disciplined system, sketching and rendering technology, multi-platform development, market compliance, engineering insights, and active collaboration.
